Just One Honest Moment

Do you like me?
Do you love me?
We’ve known each other for years, but honestly last year was my worst. Because of ego, silence, and misunderstanding, we slowly stopped talking properly.

This is our 4th year now, and a new year has begun. I don’t know what’s in your mind, but I want this year to be better. I want us to talk openly, without ignoring, without ego.

So before anything else… I just want to say sorry. Please tell me honestly how you feel about me.
